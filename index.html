About SB Jain Institute
SB Jain Institute of Technology, Management and Research (SBJITMR) is a premier NAAC "A" Grade accredited institution located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. Established in 2008 by Shri Shantilalji Badjate, the institute is affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University and approved by AICTE.

With UGC Autonomous status since 2020-21, the institute boasts a beautiful 12-acre eco-friendly campus with world-class infrastructure. SBJITMR offers cutting-edge programs in B.Tech (CSE, IT, AI/ML, Data Science, Electronics, Electrical, Mechanical), M.Tech, MBA, MCA and BCA.

About Technoutsav
Technoutsav is the Grand Annual Technical Festival of SB Jain Institute of Technology, Management and Research. It provides students a dynamic platform to showcase their technical skills, innovative ideas, and creative talents. This spectacular event brings together the brightest minds to compete, collaborate, and celebrate technology.

From exciting hackathons and coding competitions to robotics challenges and project exhibitions, Technoutsav offers diverse events for every tech enthusiast. The festival features workshops on emerging technologies like AI, Machine Learning, IoT, and Cloud Computing, led by industry experts. Join us in this celebration of innovation, creativity, and technical excellence!
